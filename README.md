# Compute Access Notes

Public notes on how startups, researchers, and open source teams can access European AI compute.

This is not legal advice. It is a public research log of what we learn about EuroHPC, the AI Factories network, IT4LIA, university partnerships, eligibility, and application requirements.

## Focus areas

- EuroHPC access paths
- AI Factories network for startups and SMEs
- IT4LIA in Italy (Leonardo, LISA, GAIA, MEGARIDE)
- University and research institute partnerships
- Legal entity requirements
- Application templates and lessons learned
